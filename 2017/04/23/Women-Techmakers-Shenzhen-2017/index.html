Women Techmakers Shenzhen 2017

一只英俊的小马
2017-04-23
activity
Event Overview

Women Techmakers Shenzhen 2017 was held on 23th April. This is the first WTM activity in Shenzhen, the topic of that day is “ Telling Our Story “ .


325 people having registered for the event , thenumber of participants is 116, inculding 85 tech women.


Our speakers come formthe most famous tech compaines in Shenzhen and Hongkong, with varied backgrouds. They shared some interesting experiences about their working intech industry , as a female.


And the other important thing is that，Organizers from Pearl River Delta are all here to see this special event.




They are organizers from Four citys in China, Hongkong, Shanghai, Shenzhen and Guangzhou,and the other one not in this photo is from Zhuhai.


Look at more photos about this event.


These three are about WTM special gifts, Organizers and volunteers are happy preparing the event.




Agenda record :

[ 13:40 - 14:00 The signboard ]


[14:00 - 14:20 introduced WTM, warm up video ]

Speaker: Summer

From Google Technology Promotion Department.


She Introduced the activity puepose about GDG and WTM. shared a variety of learning videos and translated materials.



[14:20- 14:40 Localization design ]

Speaker: Luna Zhao

Lunais a UI/UX designer form AXA.



She shares the story how she become a local designer.Not only this,but also give women who have same enthiasm towards tech a lot of advices.



[ 14:45 - 15:05 how it feels like as a female programmer work in Microsoft ]

Speaker: Serina

Serinais a very lovely girl, major in DM ( Data Mining ) and EDA.

She quote form a well-known Chinese writers Wang Xiaobo’s saying

I admit that men are different form women, but the difference not mean whichsides are stonger than the other, or smart than the other. a girl should be treat as well as a boy , she have the right to pursue what she wants. “



She have many stories inworkplace, sad or happiness . Sometimes, we are misunderstood by friends ,classmates, even parents.



[15:10 - 15:30 if you can talk to computers, you talk to people ]

Speaker: Xiaodi


As a communication instructor. She told us the influence of thinking pattern on programmers and non-progrmmers. This subject has really resonated with me.



[15:30 -15:50 Time for afternoon tea, Face to face interview]

shared the cake, fruit, took many photos.



Face to face interview from SHENZHEN SPECIAL ZONE DAILY



[15:50 - 16:10 perception and confusion of start a business in International smart hardware filed ]

Speaker: Helen Tan

Itwas the most popular speech. Helen once worked for Institute of Network Coding,The Chinese University of Hong Kong, and now is COO of Higgs Hub.



She shared expericences as a professional python progrommer, pointing out that women have to balance work-family in a way that men are not. This is a much bigger obstacle to putting more women in boardrooms.



[16:20 - 17:00 Roundtable interview]

Free talk and discuss.
